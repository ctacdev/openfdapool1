Layout Specifications

PharmaQuery is formatted with the Bootstrap framework. All content must be inside a specified column ( .xs-col-12 , .xs-sm-6 , etc) nested in a div with class of .row inside a div with a class of .container .

Site is reponsive to a screen resolution of 320px.

Content may be divisible by an outer div container with a background-color of #023151 .

Widget Specifications

Widgets on the portal page are 200px suare with a 3px corner radius.

Accessibility Inclusions

  • Font sizes must be at least 16px.
  • Non-nav links must be marked with an underline on hover.
